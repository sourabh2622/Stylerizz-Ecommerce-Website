<h1>Stylerizz</h1>

<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Stylerizz is a dynamic multi-panel eCommerce platform designed for shopping enthusiasts and style seekers. The platform bridges the gap between local vendors, stylers, and customers, offering a seamless experience for purchasing clothes, managing vendor profiles, and consulting with stylers via video calls.</p>

<hr>

<h2>Features</h2>

<h3>For Customers</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Browse and purchase clothing products.</li>
    <li>User-friendly interface for a seamless shopping experience.</li>
</ul>

<h3>For Vendors</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Manage profiles and product inventories with ease.</li>
    <li>Enhanced visibility for local vendors to expand their business reach.</li>
</ul>

<h3>For Stylers</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Offer video call consultations for personalized styling advice.</li>
    <li>Manage appointments effectively through the platform.</li>
</ul>

<h3>For Admins</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Full control of site functionalities, including vendor and product management.</li>
    <li>Ensure smooth operation of the platform.</li>
</ul>

<hr>

    <li><strong>Backend</strong>: PHP, CodeIgniter 4</li>

</ul>
